About

Hi, I'm Sreeni — your creative partner

I started Maple & Co. after spending years in a big agency and realizing the businesses that needed the most help — local shops, makers, service providers — were the ones getting the least of it.

Now I work directly with small business owners to build brands that are genuinely theirs: warm, honest, and built to last. No account managers, no handoffs — just you and me.
